Asphalt a la Mode

Description

a delectable piece of crunchy ashpalt topped with vanilla ice cream

Ingredients

  • ashpalt
  • vanilla ice cream
  • caramel drizzle (optional)

Steps

  • hammerdrill asphalt into 1 portion, roughly 5 inches by 5 inches
  • place onto serving plate
  • scoop ice cream onto ashpalt and serve immediately
