Rusty Nail Lasagna

Description

A fun twist on a savory classic

Ingredients

  • lasagna, precooked
  • carpentry nails
  • salt

Steps

  1. soak nails in heavily salted water 7 days prior to meal
  2. remove lasagna from packaging and plate 1 serving
  3. insert 15-20 rusty nails, leaving half of the nail exposed for presentation
  4. serve whenever
